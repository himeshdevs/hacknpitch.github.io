MUTUAL FUNDS


As a student we are already investing in Education and the 2nd Investment you can possibly do is that Investing in Mutual Funds. Following are the reasons why to invest in mutual funds.

For many of us its difficult to work full time while studying, so it is difficult to invest in Stock Market, Government Bonds, Real Estate as it requires Huge Sum of money. Which is very difficult to earn while studying. So Mutual Funds is the only option we have left.

Secondly we as a student you tend invest in aggressive investment options. So with some surplus money or scholarship money or pocket money one can start to invest in Mutual Funds.

One of the biggest and important rule of investment in mutual fund is “Early you start, more you Gain”. And Secondly learning the habit of saving at the early age will be beneficial for you in future...

Fourth and the last reason instead of keeping your money in saving account in bank with an annual interest of 3–4% or putting it in Fixed Deposit with an annual interest of 6–8.5% is again less as compared to some MF’s (Mutual Funds) in market. Think about the Risk. Because In every part of our life we always take risk to do new things, to experience, to learn. So “Risk is an inseparable part of our life”

SHARE MARKET

Buying sharing is buying a little part of ownership of a company. Now as any business if the business suffers loss you would be be suffering loss as well. Thus this risk of loosing money is always there.There are companies which fall down but again their are companies which show exponential growth for example Eicher Motors selling Bullet bikes have reached a very large amount of growth. Eg, TVF,MRF Thus this is risky but again the returns are also quite high. FUN FACT: If you invested Rs 10,000 in Wipro 40 years back now it would have been worth Rs 70 Crore.

There is a large difference between shares. There are shares which do not increase in years and often fall but then suddenly increase in a short span of time. Then again there are shares which steadily increase over the years. But we generally never invest in a single share. When investing we diversify thus creating a portfolio of stocks i.e. investing in at least 8-10 companies. This acts as a backup if any of our investments fail. Thus with a basket of stocks we can expect about 18-20% returns in long run (min 5 years). This would hold true especially for a High Growth Economy like the Indian Economy. Thus the returns for Stock Market is about 3x more than FD and 2x more than Gold/Real Estate.Now even in low growth economies there are people who picked their stocks as such that they were able to get about 30% growth rate. Thus in a country like India, expecting about 20% returns is realistic tough the stock picking needs to good as well.

In stock market the selection of stocks with consistent returns is more important than the total amount of capital invested. Eg. This was also told to some Charter Accountants who are known to be extremely knowledgeable in commerce stuff. The shocking result was that after 5 years the shares picked by the kids outperformed the shares picked by the CAs. Now a theory on how this happened: The kids chose the brands in which they were interested, brands like Cadbury, Disney, Barbie, the kids were customers of the products of these companies and thus knew the products they invested in best. CRYPTOCURRENCY

The edge of the youth in any industry, including crypto trading, is the abundance of time. Students have more freedom to learn more about the market and make trades with potentially large gains. As a matter of fact, a percentage of college students already trade in the crypto market today. Guided with techniques such crypto arbitrage, some are already earning far more than how much a regular tertiary student can make. If you are a student looking for a shot in altcoins, trades, and the like, keep reading. Getting Started: Preparations Before Trading Crypto Do these preliminary steps first before delving deeper into the market.

Separate the email address you use for crypto and for your personal life. This is to ensure cybersecurity as you hop into the market. Protonmail guarantees high-level security for the address that you can use when trading and buying. Part of this is setting a strong password. Use a combination of random letters with random capitalizations mixed with random numbers. You may also opt to use two-factor authentication to even double up your account’s safety and security.

Select a crypto wallet that is suited to your needs, goals in trading. The most popular types of wallets are software wallets (hot wallets), hardware wallets, and paper wallets. Hot wallets are considered the most vulnerable type, as these are connected to the internet (e.g., web wallets, mobile wallets, desktop wallets). Hardware wallets can be pricey, but they provide medium security. Paper wallets are considered the most secure since they are not connected to the cloud, but they can be highly inconvenient.

For beginners, it is advisable to start with popular and major currencies such as Bitcoin Cash, Ripple, Litecoin, and Ethereum. These have been longstanding across exchange platforms and are relatively stable, despite the innate volatility of the market.

Never make an arbitrary decision if you want to earn, or at least minimize potential losses in trading. There are many advantageous mechanisms you can capitalize on, including smart trading platforms and automated trading bots. A well-known tactic is cryptocurrency arbitrage, which follows the principle of exploiting market inefficiency and inconsistency. It is about using the different prices of exchanges to your advantage. It takes precise timing to make trades that can generate high-yielding returns and profit.

Traders cannot be rash when deciding to buy and sell on an exchange. Every move must be smart so as to evade losses and alleviate risks.Consider a better manual trading experience through its Smart Trading Platform. Its design should uphold both functionality and simplicity. Users should be able to efficiently plan trades from start to finish. Bots remain a top choice for beginner and experienced traders alike because of incredibly powerful and intelligent trading bots. Users can connect bots to trading views and can automatically trigger commands from there. This is highly convenient for students who do not have the time and experienced judgment to make manual trades. Developers continuously improve and develop trading bots by the day with the support and creative ideation of a vibrant community.

• Only use the money you can afford to use. Instead of allotting extra money to senseless parties and fun, perhaps you can invest your disposable income into something more productive like crypto trading. Be mindful of your cybersecurity. The crypto market is decentralized, so you have to independently sort your issues in terms of security and protect yourself from theft and fraud.Use trading tools. Make use of trading tools, as they can greatly jumpstart your trading experience — both manual and automated. The future of trading is digital. It is good that more and more people make themselves aware of how to use and operate it today.
